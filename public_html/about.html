ABOUT

Fukuoka Package USA, Inc.  has taken over business from K&H Pacific Inc., which has been importing and wholesaling food containers in Hawaii and the West Coast for about 40 years.  We will incorporate the experience and history of K&H Pacific Inc.  into our mission to provide quality products and services to our valued customers.  Fukuoka Package USA, Inc.  supplies various food packaging products, specializing in takeout containers such as Sushi and Bento trays and other restaurant supplies.  Please feel free to ask about our products and services.
